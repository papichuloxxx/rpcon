About RP Builders

BUILDING ZIMBABWE'S FUTURE SINCE 2016

Real Providers Building Contractors began as a general works contractor in 2016. We have evolved from solo building projects into a strong construction and development company, growing dramatically in both size and reputation.

Today, we operate as a complete professional construction service, using our collective skills and understanding of markets to develop unique value propositions for all our clients - from small residential builds to major civil engineering works.

Our Mission

To promote property development and investment in Zimbabwe using innovative and environmentally friendly solutions.

Our Vision

To be synonymous with excellence in overall project delivery - every project, every time.

Our Values

WHAT DRIVES US

Five core principles guide every project we undertake and every relationship we build.

Empowerment

Committed to developing substantial opportunity and participation for minority, women and disabled veterans business enterprises. We work with qualified and capable subcontractors and suppliers.

Collaborative Culture

A culture of collaboration and cooperation. We listen to our clients, subcontractors and vendors to discover the best means to provide an outstanding construction experience.

Sustainability

Giving back to the communities in which we work is a long-standing tradition. We provide donations of time, money, materials and expertise, and encourage employee volunteering.

Safety First

"Safely - the only way to work!" That is the motto by which we live. No task is so important that we cannot find a safe way to do it. Safety is central to everything we do.

Environment

Committed to minimum environmental impact through the structures we construct and the methods we employ. We strive to cater for all our energy needs through renewables.

Excellence

Implementing cutting-edge technology on our projects while maintaining successful working relationships. Excellence is not an act - it is our standard.
